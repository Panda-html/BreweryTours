About Us

Aussie Brewery Tours specialises in small public tours and customizable private events with VIP access to the breweries and cideries of Victoria

Our public tours leave daily from Melbourne with hotel pick-ups available. We can also customise a tour for your next corporate event, bucks or hens party, birthday or sporting/social club event. If you dont have a full day, or prefer something different, ask us about our food, beer, cider and wine matching dinners.

Our expert guides and emcees will entertain you with a unique take on beer history and culture, whilst you eat and drink the day away. From pilsners to pale ales, apple and pear ciders, through porters and stouts we will take you on a tour of the world’s best beer and cider styles, created locally by the passionate brewers, cider and winemakers of Victoria.
